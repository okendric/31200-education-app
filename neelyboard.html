ability

Ability

Having talent or skill to do something.

"I have the ability to balance a basketball on my finger!"

absorb

Absorb

Take in or soak up a liquid/energy/substance.

"Use that sponge to absorb the water on the car."

access

Access

To enter or approach a place.

"I unlocked the door, now I can access this room."

accuse

Accuse

Charge a person with an offense or crime.

"Don't accuse me of cheating because I didn't!"

act

Act

Take action or do something.

"The boy had to act strong so he wasn't afraid."

active

Active

A sporty person, to be hardworking, or for something to be functional.

"That kid is active because he plays sports!"

actual

Actual

A fact that is intended, expected, or believed.

"The actual color of a red apple is red."

adopt

Adopt

To bring it up as one's own.

"The puppy didn't have a home, so the boy adopted it."

advantage

Advantage

A condition where a person has a favorable or superior position.

"Since he runs a lot, he has an advantage for winning the race."

advice

Advice

Guidance or recommendations for a future action.

"Here's some advice, if you turn in all your homework, you will get good grades versus if you didn't."

ambition

Ambition

A strong desire to do or achieve something.

"A good example of ambition is to never give up, always keep going!"

ancient

Ancient

Belonging to the past a long time ago and may not exist anymore.

"There are some buildings created years ago that are so ancient!"

approach

Approach

Come near or closer to someone/something either by distance or time.

"This cute koala slowly approached me because I had some food for it!"

arrange

Arrange

To put things in a neat, attractive, or required order.

"All of these book are arranged by their colors."

arctic

Arctic

Very cold weather conditions, also areas near the North Pole.

"In the arctic, you can see animals like penguins and polar bears!"

attitude

Attitude

A way of thinking about someone or something.

"The boy had a bad attitude because his parents wouldn't buy him a toy."

attract

Attract

Cause things to come together or cause someone to have a liking for something.

"If you use a magnet near nails, it will attract the nails to it."

average

Average

The midpoint, common value, or standard for a value/quality.

"When you go to a dollar store, the average price for items is only a dollar!"

avoid

Avoid

To keep away or stop yourself/someone from doing something.

"If you avoid watching T.V. when doing your homework, it might help you focus better!"
